$1.8 Million Verdict for Truck Accident Victim

Home/Blog/Case Result

Verdict
October 28, 2024
Michael Anderson, Esq.

A speeding tractor-trailer rear-ended our client on the highway, causing a traumatic brain injury and months of missed work. After the insurance company denied responsibility, our trial team proved negligence and secured a $1.8 million jury verdict to cover medical care, lost wages, and future rehabilitation.

Accident scene documentation showing vehicle damage

We preserved critical black box data, obtained the driver's logs, and partnered with accident reconstruction experts to demonstrate how fatigue and excessive speed caused the crash. That evidence shifted the case from dispute to clear liability in front of the jury.

Building the Evidence That Wins

Our team interviewed eyewitnesses, documented the scene, and coordinated with neurologists to explain the lifelong impact of the brain injury. By presenting future care costs and vocational losses, we showed the full value of the claim beyond initial medical bills.

Legal team reviewing case evidence and documentation

Damages included past and future medical expenses, pain and suffering, and loss of earning capacity. We also sought punitive damages for the company's disregard of safety protocols, underscoring the importance of corporate accountability.

Every personal injury case is unique. Deadlines apply, evidence can disappear quickly, and results depend on the specific facts and jurisdiction. Early legal help is key to preserving your rights.

We handle truck, car, motorcycle, and other serious injury cases on a contingency fee basis. You pay nothing upfront, and no attorney's fee unless we recover compensation on your behalf.

If you or a loved one was hurt in a commercial vehicle crash, contact Lion Law for a free consultation. Results may vary based on the facts of your case; past outcomes do not guarantee future results.
