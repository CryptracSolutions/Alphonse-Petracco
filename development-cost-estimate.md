## Key Cost Drivers

1. **Content/Copywriting (35-40% of cost)** - 45,000+ words of professional copy, SEO-optimized, jurisdiction-specific
2. **AI Chatbot (15-20%)** - Streaming responses, context-aware, OpenAI integration with page scraping
3. **Design System (15-20%)** - Sophisticated CSS with 18 color tokens, animations, responsive breakpoints
4. **FAQ System** - Most complex page: search, filtering, deep-linking, category tabs, accordion
5. **Accessibility** - Full ARIA support, keyboard navigation throughout
